Company Overview
30 Years of Engineering Excellence

Established in 1995, Kurlekar Engineering Works is a professionally managed manufacturing unit based in Malad West, Mumbai. For over three decades, we have specialised in precision sheet metal press components — building deep tooling expertise and a reputation for consistent, reliable supply.

We serve OEMs and Tier-2 manufacturers across the electric motor, fan, and automotive sectors — manufacturing components exactly as per customer drawings or approved samples.

Our strength lies in in-house die making, stringent process control, and a long-term partnership approach — giving customers a single accountable partner from first article through volume delivery.
