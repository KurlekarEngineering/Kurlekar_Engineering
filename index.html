Who We Are

BUILT ON
DECADES OF

Kurlekar Engineering Works is a professionally managed manufacturing unit based in Mumbai, specialising in precision sheet metal press components. With decades of hands-on press shop experience and deep tooling expertise, we serve OEMs and Tier-2 manufacturers across critical industrial sectors.

Our strength lies in consistent quality, stringent process control, and the ability to manufacture components exactly as per customer drawings or approved samples.
